Asia Mai


Asia Mai, an Ohio native, is an inspired freelancing artist. Her mother says that dance is the air that she breathes. She has been a student and performer of dance for over twenty years, an instructor of dance over 13 years, and has worked professionally as a choreographer for 4 years.
Asia trained statewide in Ohio’s premier pre-professional dance studios. Daryl Jervis Dance Studios, Pinnell Dance Center, and The Dance Centre by Heidi Glynias which taught her lasting core principles of dance. From the age of 8, she has danced competitively and traveled across country cultivating her craft under the industry’s leading choreographers. Asia also studied dance collegiately at Ohio University in Athens, Ohio, where, the curriculum focused on performance, the study of technique, and the study of choreography. Currently, Asia is choreographing and instructing at Audacious Art Studio


Amy Houska


For over 30 years Amy, taught students the joy of being a muscian. Her biggest joy is seeing her students blossom . She prides herself in teaching each student a strong foundational musical education consisting of note reading, tab reading, chord reading, ear training, rhythm studies, scales, theory, musical construction and improvisation. Her students have earned scholarships and went on to have careers in music. Amy was born in Washington D.C and has 12 years of performance experience. She is currently performing as a solo act and teaching at Audacious Art studio


Marina Buschev

Marina was a principal dancer in the Armenian National Theatre of Ballet and Opera for ten years and graduated from the School of Choreography in Armenia. She has more than fifteen years of experience as a ballet dancer, and has performed in many ballets such as Nutcracker, Giselle, Don Quixote, Le Bayadere, Paquita, Gayane, Les Sylphydes, Le Corsaire among many others. She has been a dance instructor since 2010 teaching ballet to children and adults in all aspects of dance, preparation and performance. She has a comprehensive knowledge of all forms of dance and loves to teach young children.


Phillip Wright


Phil began learning to play the saxophone entering middle school. He was born and raised in Cleveland, OH. The passion and dedication from his instructors inspired him pursue and continue with music to adulthood. He earned a B.M. in Saxophone Performance and Music Theory in 2015 from Ohio University. In May 2017, he earned his M.M. in Saxophone Performance. Throughout his education in music, Phil has accrued a wide experience performing in both classical and jazz ensembles.
