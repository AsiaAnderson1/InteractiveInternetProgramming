Dance Courses

Heels class with Asia

Dance a sultry dance routine in heels! It's fun! This is an OPEN level heels class led by choreographer and instructor Asia Mai. Attend class for one hour beginning at 7PM on Monday nights. Blending contemporary technique with hip hop movement, Audacious Heels provides a vibrant, sensual, and distinct class experience! Don't worry heels are optional!

Attire:

Wear whatever makes you feel bold and comfortable! Heels that you feel comfortable moving in (or sneakers).

Cost of Attendance: $10


Hip Hop with Asia

This is an open level Hip Hop class! Dance to today's hip hop beats! It's fun! Learn from the rhythms of choreographer and instructor Asia Mai. Attend class for one hour beginning at 7PM on Thursday nights.

Attire:

Wear sporty loose clothing!

Cost of Attendance: $10


Beginner Ballet with Marina

Dance a sultry dance routine in heels! It's fun! This is an OPEN level ballet class led by choreographer and instructor Marina. Attend class for one hour beginning at 7PM on Tuesday nights. You will begin with a warm-up at the barre, learn center-floor work, and end with across-the-floor work.

Attire:

Ballet shoes are a must. Leotard are optional. However, must wear form fitting clothing in order for instructor to see ballet lines.

Cost of Attendance: $10


Music courses


Jazz with Phil

Interested in learning the music composition of Jazz. Take a 45 minute course with Philip Wright. This is a novice friendly course aimed at teaching the basics of Jazz music. Class is held Saturday mornings.

Required:

Bring something to take notes with.

Cost of Attendance: $10


Strings with Amy

Ever wanted to learn how to play guitar? Take a course with Amy Houska. No experience is needed. Our lessons are group lessons and aimed to provide a stress-free zone for beginners. Each lesson is 45 minutes long and conducted Wednesday nights at 8PM.

Required:

Bring your own instrument.

Cost of Attendance: $10
